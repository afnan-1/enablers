Local Content

New policies to support and favor the local product:

  • Price preference policy for the national product
  • A mandatory list of national products
  • Policies of the minimum required for local content from contracting companies
  • Include a weight for local content in the financial evaluation, except for supply contracts
  • Fee exemption for authorized imports for industrial Companies (such as raw materials, machinery and equipment)

Financial Enablement

Several programs and products targeting the program sectors:

  • Increase the capital of the Saudi Industrial Development Fund to finance all program sectors
  • “Tanafusya” program, which provides financing and advisory services to raise the competitiveness of the local product and improve productivity.
  • "Tawteen" program to support and enhance the trend of increasing spending on local content and to facilitate. Promote and finance supply chain localization opportunities
  • Working capital financing product.
  • Multipurpose financing product.
  • Opening Letters of Credit.
  • Acquisition financing product.

Human Capital

Several programs to support the development of the workforce:

  • “Mumken" program to provide training support to private sector enterprises through specialized training programs to raise the skills of the workforce to keep pace with the current need.
  • Employment support program through temporary coverage of Saudi manpower costs
